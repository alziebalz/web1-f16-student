ABOUT

Co-owner of the antiques, taxidermy and novelty shop The Strange & Unusual Oddities Parlor who is also an award-winning tattoo artist who has been featured in Inked Magazine and Freshly Inked. She has a private and appointment-only tattoo studio in the parlor.


BEFORE FAME

She earned a degree in fashion design from the Fashion Institute of Technology in 2007 and was named the Critic's Choice award winner of her graduating class.


TRIVIA

She worked as a designer and artist for a private label clothing company in New York City until 2011.


FAMILY LIFE

She got engaged to her longtime boyfriend, keyboardist Josh Balz, in 2016. Together they own The Strange & Unusual Oddities Parlor.


ASSOCIATED WITH

Balz performs alongside Chris Motionless in the band Motionless in White.
